## What is Ghostfolio?

Ghostfolio empowers investors to make data-driven decisions by consolidating all their holdings in one place. Whether you trade stocks, ETFs, or cryptocurrencies across multiple platforms, Ghostfolio provides a unified view of your wealth with performance metrics, portfolio composition, and risk analysis.

The application is designed for personal use in continuous operation — it tracks your portfolio in real time and surfaces actionable insights without requiring you to maintain a spreadsheet.

## Technology stack

Ghostfolio is a full-stack TypeScript application organized as an [Nx](https://nx.dev) monorepo:

- **Backend**: [NestJS](https://nestjs.com) with [PostgreSQL](https://www.postgresql.org) (via [Prisma](https://www.prisma.io)) and [Redis](https://redis.io) for caching
- **Frontend**: [Angular](https://angular.dev) with [Angular Material](https://material.angular.io)
- **Deployment**: Docker-based with official images for `linux/amd64`, `linux/arm/v7`, and `linux/arm64`

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [ghostfol.io](https://ghostfol.io) or deploy Ghostfolio on your own server. The first registered user automatically receives the `ADMIN` role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your accounts">
    Create brokerage accounts under **My Ghostfolio → Accounts** and link them to platforms like Interactive Brokers, Coinbase, or any custom platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import your activities">
    Add transactions manually, import a CSV file, or use the REST API to bulk import your trading history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your portfolio">
    Navigate to the **Portfolio** section to see your holdings, performance charts, diversification breakdown, and risk analysis.
  </Step>
</Steps>
